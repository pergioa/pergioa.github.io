# Health Companion Super App
## UX/UI Case Study

**Author:** Sergio Abreo Alvarez
**Date:** February 2025

---

## Project Overview

A mobile app designed to help individuals with chronic health conditions manage their medications and doctor appointments. The app allows users to set reminders, track medication usage, and communicate with healthcare professionals.

### Problem Statement

THIS IS A WORK IN PROGESS

### Goals

- Help users manage medication schedules effectively
- Simplify doctor appointment booking and reminders
- Enable communication with healthcare providers
- Track health progress over time
